GENERAL KEYS
------------------

ESCAPE
 -quits demo

C
 -toggles free cam mode

I or F1
 -toggles info screen

R
 -reset timeline

F2
 -appends all the current parameters to parameter_hierarchy.txt

SPACE
 -pauses the demo

COMMA
 -rewinds 1 sec

SHIFT+COMMA
 -rewinds 1 / 30 sec (1 frame)

CTLR+COMMA
 -rewinds 3 sec

PERIOD
 -forwards 1 sec

SHIFT+PERIOD
 -forwards 1 / 30 sec (1 frame)

CTLR+PERIOD
 -forwards 3 sec

RETURN
 -adds sync points to sync.txt


POST PROCESSING KEYS
--------------------

N+
-add noise strength

N-
-negate noise strength

Tone Mapping Adapted luminance
Y+
 -add adapted luminance
Y-
 -negate adapted luminance

Tone mapping saturation
ST+
 -add saturation
ST-
 -negate saturation

Tone mapping whitepoint
W+
 -add whitepoint in tonemap
W-
 -negate whitepoint in tonemap

Bloom brightpass filter adapted luminance
L+
 -add bloom luminance
L-
 -negate bloom luminance

Bloom brightpass treshold
BT+
 -add bloom brightpass treshold
BT-
 -negate bloom brightpass treshold

Bloom brightpass offset
BO+
 -add bloom brightpass offset
BO-
 -negate bloom brightpass offset

Bloom whitepoint
BW+
 -add bloom whitepoint
BW-
 -negate bloom whitepoint
